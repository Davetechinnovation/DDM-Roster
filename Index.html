Note:  Each rooms task/duty should be reshuflled weekly
ie. if on wednesday Dandave washes the toilet, and Mr Nzube fetches water. Then the upper wednesday, it reshuffles therby, Mr Nzube now washes the toilet and Dandave fetches the water.

Other Information: Sunday being the day of church for most people, Dandave has volunteered to take up the responsibility of EACH SUNDAY, to avoid inconviniences, or Excuse that may occur.
